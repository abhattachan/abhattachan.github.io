Abinash Bhattachan

Assistant Professor

California State University East Bay

Welcome to the Climate Health Interactions Lab (CHIL) at Cal State East Bay in the Department of Earth and Environmental Sciences.

Before joining CSUEB, I was a researcher in the Department of Geography at UCLA (2017-2020). I worked with Greg Okin and Dennis Lettenmaier on a project focused on studying the effects of climate variability on infectious diseases (West Nile virus and Valley Fever) in California.

Please visit our research page to learn more about our work. We currently have opportunities for motivated undergraduate and graduate students in our research group. More information about the department’s MS program is available here.
